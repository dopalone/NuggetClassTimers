Spell Cheat Sheet

Using NCT is simple, treat it like a bit different ERT notes. How to actually use it?

This how, for example: 10 Psu rwoff

Let's break it down.
10 - a timer, type in how many seconds after combat starts you want your timer to 'pop'.
Psu - Pain Suppression, NCT uses 3-letter codes to recognize spells, you can put max two spells into each timer.
rwoff - a toggle, put ALWAYS at the end of a timer. 'off' means there will be no BW/DBM style text warning in the middle of the screen, 'on' means the opposite.
