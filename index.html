Spell Cheat Sheet

Using DopaReminders is simple, treat it like a bit different ERT notes. How to actually use it?

Example: 10 Psu Pwb 5 rwoff

Let's break it down:
10 - timer in seconds after combat starts.
Psu - spell shortcut, max two spells per timer.
Pwb - spell shortcut, max two spells per timer.
After one or two spells you can write anything you want your message to have. Remember to always put it before a number and rwon/rwoff.
5 - Amount of seconds you want your bar displayed before the timer hits. Leave empty for it to default to 5 seconds.
rwoff - toggle for BW/DBM style text warnings ('on' shows warning, 'off' hides it).
